Mumbai's Best Joint Replacement Surgeon

Dr. Vinay S. Joshi · Lead Arthroplasty Surgeon · Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital

Dr. Vinay S. Joshi is Mumbai's foremost consultant orthopaedic surgeon. He leads the Arthroplasty & Joint Replacement team at Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital as its most senior joint replacement specialist — heading a world-class surgical team in one of India's premier hospitals.

He spent 12 years training at five of the United Kingdom's finest orthopaedic centres, with additional fellowships in Canada, Italy, Singapore and the USA. Since joining KDAH in July 2012, he has performed more than 4,500 joint replacements using minimally invasive techniques — exclusively primary and complex revision surgeries.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions About Joint Replacement

You may be a candidate if you have persistent joint pain limiting daily activities, significant stiffness, or non-surgical treatments are no longer providing relief. Dr. Joshi will assess your X-rays, clinical history, and functional status to advise on the best approach.
Robotic-assisted surgery uses real-time intra-operative bone tracking and robotic guidance to position the implant with sub-millimetre accuracy — with no pre-operative CT scan required. The VELYS system maps the patient's anatomy live during surgery itself. This results in better alignment, improved functional outcomes, and a more natural-feeling joint. Dr. Joshi uses the VELYS robotic system for eligible patients at KDAH.
Most patients walk with assistance the day after surgery. Average hospital stay is 5 days. Light activities resume at 4–6 weeks; full recovery at 3–6 months.
A high-flex knee implant allows bending up to 150° — essential for Indian patients who sit cross-legged, kneel for prayer, or squat. Dr. Joshi specialises in high-flex knee replacements to ensure patients resume traditional activities comfortably.
